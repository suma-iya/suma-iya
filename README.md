## `< About Me />`

</div>

I'm a Computer Science & Engineering student at **Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET)**, driven by a deep passion for Artificial Intelligence and its potential to solve real-world problems. My work sits at the intersection of **Machine Learning**, **Reinforcement Learning**, and **Software Engineering research** — from designing lightweight neural networks for medical imaging to building RL agents that learn personalized nutrition plans grounded in Bangladesh-specific food data.

When I'm not reading papers or running experiments, I'm building **backend systems** — designing clean REST APIs, thinking through distributed architecture, and working with cloud-native tooling. I have a genuine love for algorithmic problem-solving that has taken me to the **ICPC Asia Dhaka Regional Contest** and sharpened my ability to write fast, correct code under pressure.

I care about knowledge sharing too. I actively **mentor junior programmers**, helping them navigate competitive programming and research, because I believe the best way to deepen your own understanding is to teach.
